Our Journey of Innovation

For over a decade, ADXPI has been at the forefront of digital transformation, helping businesses worldwide harness the power of technology.

Our Humble Beginnings

Founded in 2010 by technology visionaries Sarah Johnson and Michael Chen, ADXPI started as a small IT consulting firm in San Francisco. With just five employees and a single client, we set out with a mission to make technology work for businesses, not against them.

Our breakthrough came in 2013 when we developed a revolutionary cloud migration framework that reduced implementation times by 40%. This innovation established ADXPI as a leader in cloud solutions and set us on a path of rapid growth.

Today, ADXPI has grown into a global technology solutions provider with over 10,000 professionals serving clients in 20+ countries. Despite our growth, we remain committed to our founding principles of innovation, excellence, and client success.
