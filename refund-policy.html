Refund & Cancellation Policy

Understand how ADXPI handles order cancellations, refunds, and related requests.

1. Overview

ADXPI Technologies strives to ensure complete satisfaction with our services. This Refund & Cancellation Policy explains the circumstances under which cancellations or refunds may be processed.

2. Service Cancellation

Clients can request cancellation of any service prior to the project commencement or resource allocation. Once work has started, cancellations will be considered on a case-by-case basis depending on progress and effort spent.

  • Requests for cancellation must be made in writing via email to billing@adxpi.com.
  • Approved cancellations before project initiation will receive a full refund minus transaction charges, if any.

3. Refund Conditions

Refunds are issued only under the following conditions:

  • Duplicate payment due to technical error.
  • Service not delivered due to unforeseen internal reasons.
  • Any valid reason determined solely by ADXPI management.

Refund requests will not be accepted for change-of-mind scenarios or once significant project progress has been made.
